From Petri Dish to Plate

HyPhyve is the education arm of the Hyphal ecosystem. We build approachable, standards-aligned curriculum and hands-on labs so students can culture, clone, expand, and fruit gourmet mushrooms safely and repeatably.

Our kits and guides cover sterilization, culture preservation, spawn expansion, bulk substrate selection, and multiple fruiting formats.
